Clownfish

A layer of mucus on the clownfish skin makes it immune to the fish-eating anemone's lethal sting.

Bright orange with three distinctive white bars, clown anemonefish are among the most recognizable of all reef-dwellers.

  • Scientific Name: Amphiprioninae
  • Average Length: 11 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 6 - 10 years
  • Habitat: Tropical coral reefs
  • Diet: Carnivore
